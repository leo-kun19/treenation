Our Services

1. Upcycling Service, Send Us Your Clothes!

Turn your old clothing into something completely new. Choose a style, send us your pieces, and we'll redesign them into custom, sustainable fashion made just for you.

3. Repair and Recycle

Extend the life of your favorite garments. We offer resizing, patching, fixing tears, and refreshing old clothes to make them wearable again.

4. Fabric Donating Program

Donate clothing or fabric scraps you no longer need. We will upcycle them into future collections and reduce textile waste.
